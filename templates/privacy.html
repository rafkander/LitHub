Privacy

Kzoo LitHub uses cookies to keep track of log in status.

Kzoo LitHub is a service provided without any warranty. However, we try to ensure that your privacy is respected and that your private data is not disclosed.

Kzoo LitHub retains data about books that you have "unlisted" after they have been unlisted. However, all your data is deleted when you delete your account.

Kzoo LitHub stores passwords only as salted hashes. Therefore, in the unlikely event that the Kzoo LitHub database is compromised, there is an additional layer of security securing your password.

Facebook Login Privacy Policy

Kzoo LitHub offers a Facebook Login feature that allows users to login to Kzoo LitHub with their Facebook accounts. The information that Kzoo LitHub gets from Facebook is described below.

  • If you associate your Kzoo LitHub and Facebook accounts, we store your Facebook user id.
  • Kzoo LitHub may also access and store your first and last name, your email address and your Facebook networks to verify your identity.
  • Kzoo LitHub also requests permission to push Open Graph activities to Facebook. You may deny this setting while authorizing the Kzoo LitHub Facebook App. You may also change your settings directly on Facebook.

Kzoo LitHub cannot and will not store your Facebook password.
