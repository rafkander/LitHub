Why should you use LitHub?

LitHub can help you save money! When compared to websites online and bookstores, LitHub helps buy books for less and sell them for more. Here's how:

If you buy a Calculus book for $100 and later sell it, you will probably get far less than $100 in return. This is because there are additional costs in buying and selling a book through a bookstore or website. First, the company needs to make money and keeps a portion of the total sales. Second, the book needs to be packaged and shipped. As a result, you pay more than the book's real "value" when you buy it and make less when you sell it.

How LitHub works

If books are bought and sold by the people who actually use the books (true free-market style) there are no extra fees or costs. You buy and sell at the same going rate. LitHub essentially helps you network and find people willing to sell the books you want and buy the books you want to sell.

The Kzoo LitHub isn't guaranteed to be cheaper and doesn't guarantee that you will find a seller or buyer, since there are only a limited number of people who use this website. Comparing prices is always a good idea.

Features

  • Facebook integration: one click login (no username or password); register in under 20 seconds!
  • Google book preview: "look" at (and even inside) a book before you buy! (Available for many books)
  • ISBN lookup: auto-populated book information based on the ISBN when you sell a book.
  • HTML5 speech recognition on search forms (currently only on Google Chrome)
  • Restricted to K college students
