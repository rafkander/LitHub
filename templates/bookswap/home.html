Kzoo LitHub helps you save money

Book stores can be expensive. Trading websites have shipping charges when you buy, fees and commissions when you sell. As a campus, we already have many of the book that we need for the next quarter. When we buy and sell to each other, we save money on charges, fees and commissions.

How it works

When you find a book on Kzoo LitHub, you contact the seller and meet on-campus to trade the book. If a book is not available, you may "subscribe" to the book and receive a notification when becomes available.

Since the buyer and seller meet in person, using Kzoo LitHub is completely free of cost.

About LitHub

The Kalamazoo College Computer Science Club develops and maintains the Kzoo LitHub website. LitHub is an open source project, licensed under the GNU GPLv3. You can modify and distribute the source code under the conditions of the license. Other colleges and communities are free to download the source code and run their own LitHub.
